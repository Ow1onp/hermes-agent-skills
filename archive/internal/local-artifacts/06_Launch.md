## Key Messaging Pillars

All 6 platforms hit these 6 selling points:
1. **Hermes Agent** — native deep integration
2. **Agent Skills Standard** — open standard compliance
3. **Skill Validator** — 6-dimension quality gate
4. **CLI Toolchain** — `hermes-skill create/validate/list`
5. **SOUL.md Support** — persona-aware skill adaptation
6. **Self-Evolving Skills** — EvolutionEngine (Hermes-unique)

## Tone Guidelines Applied

- No hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
- No fake urgency ("limited time", "act now")
- No disparagement of competing projects
- Each platform's tone independently optimized (Reddit = personal story, HN = debate, PH = product showcase, Discord = community announcement)
